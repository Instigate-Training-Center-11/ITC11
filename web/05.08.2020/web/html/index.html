♥ Why us?


Moments…

Life is not about how many breaths you take, but about how many moments in life take your breath away…”

At “Your Day” Weddings & Events, Inc., we are focused on providing ‘Start to Finish’ Wedding Planning, Coordination, and Design services with the highest levels of customer satisfaction & we will go above and beyond to do everything we can to meet your expectations, and beyond!

Tell Your Story ♥

We want you to tell us about your dream event in detail so we can help you put together your perfect vision. With the information you provide us we will generate an estimate fee, sign a contract and begin our journey!

Save Your Date ♥

Once we’ve collected information like, number of guests, budget and use of venue we can start planning what days this event will take place by creating a draft program of the main events!

Find Your Location ♥

If you are unable to travel to see the locations we will send you a bespoke presentation of the venues suggested, making it simple to choose the right place for your ceremony and party!

Plan Your Details ♥

Add little touches of personality and style to your event by choosing some extra services like catering, stationery, accommodations, decorations, and photography to bring your event together!

Your Final Steps ♥

Your invitations will be sent out with the appropriate information for all your guests and your vendors will be confirmed with your deposit payments all in order!

Enjoy Your Event ♥

With everything organized and planned out you can relax and enjoy your event. Venice First will accompany you throughout the entire day for constant support so you can have peace of mind knowing everything is running perfectly and your guests are happy!
